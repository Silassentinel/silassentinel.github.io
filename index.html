BRUTALIST WEB DESIGN

Brutalism in web design is a crude, plain, and transparent style that prioritizes functionality over form. It's characterized by its raw appearance and extremely simplistic and minimalist approach.

"While brutalist websites might not have the megalithic and severe presences that brutalist buildings do, they are constructed in a similar fashion using the raw materials of the web."

Raw Structure

Brutalism showcases the raw structure of a website, similar to how brutalist architecture exposes structural elements. The design strips away decorative elements to reveal the underlying framework.

Monospace Fonts

System fonts and monospaced typography are hallmarks of brutalist design. They emphasize the raw, unpolished aesthetic that brutalism embraces.

High Contrast

Bold, high-contrast designs with sharp edges and geometric components help create the industrial feel of brutalist websites.
